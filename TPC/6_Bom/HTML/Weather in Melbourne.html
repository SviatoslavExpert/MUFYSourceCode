Weather in Melbourne
Infogram

Introduction

The graph displays the weather in Melbourne every month for each year.


Graph type and purpose

A line graph is used to show the change in temperature every month for each year.


Data included

The weather of each year are represented by lines with different colours.


Main finding/trend

The main finding in the trend is that the year 2015 has the highest and lowest temperature among the other years.


Other findings

The month with the lowest temperature is July while December has the highest temperature.


Source of data

The source data came from Wikipedia, their website’s Address is http://www.bom.gov.au/jsp/ncc/cdio/weatherData/av?p_nccObsCode=122&p_display_type=dailyDataFile&p_startYear=2017&p_c=-1490943699&p_stn_num=086338


Conclusion

Overall, the graph shows that the year 2015 has the lowest and highest temperature.
